Foundation 9 Mathematics

Foundation batches, especially for students in classes 9th, and 10th, are designed to lay a strong groundwork in subjects like Science and Mathematics. These programs aim to prepare students for competitive exams such as IIT JEE (Indian Institutes of Technology Joint Entrance Examination) and NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test), which are entrance exams for engineering and medical colleges, respectively. The emphasis on early learning helps build a solid foundation for these challenging exams.
